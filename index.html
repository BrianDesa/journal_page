The Ceylon Journal of Science is a peer-reviewed academic journal published by the University of Peradeniya. The journal promotes scientific research and scholarly communication across multiple disciplines.


Explore Journal

ABOUT

The Ceylon Journal of Science publishes high-quality research articles, review papers, and scholarly communications across scientific disciplines. The journal aims to promote innovative research and academic collaboration both locally and internationally.

All articles are officially hosted and maintained on the university's journal management platform.

EDITORIAL BOARD

The editorial board consists of distinguished academics and researchers contributing to the quality and direction of the journal.
